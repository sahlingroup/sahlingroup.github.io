Open positions

Postdocs

I'm looking to hire one or two postdocs in Computational Biology (details below). The call will be open untill the positions are filled.

The group

We are located in Stockholm at the Science for Life Laboratory, one of Sweden's most prestigious research institutes. We have acquired funding from competitive funding bodies, and we regularly publish in high-impact venues (see my Google Scholar profile). Our research interests broadly span algorithm development and analysis of sequence data. We have ongoing projects on method development for rapid sequence mapping and sequence similarity searches, various computational problems in transcriptomic analysis, and collaborative projects in genome assembly. We also consider your project proposals if it aligns with the group's research programs.

Qualifications

It is preferred if applicants have experience in relevant areas such as algorithms and data structures for genomic or transcriptomic analysis, or have developed efficient software for analyzing high-throughput sequencing data.  The ideal applicant has:
  • A Ph.D. in Computer Science
  • Strong skills in at least one of the following languages: C/C++, Rust, Python
  • Published papers in English
  • Experience with analyzing sequencing data
  • Good communication skills

To apply

To apply, please send a letter of interest and current CV to ksahlin@math-.su.se. In addition, for qualified applicants, I might ask you for a couple of recommendation letters or their contact details. Applications will be reviewed until the positions are filled. Questions should be directed to me at ksahlin@math-.su.se.

BSc and MSc thesis projects

Contact me for eventual Master's or Bachelors's projects within algorithms in Life Sciences and Bioinformatics. Please mention your academic background and include the courses you took and your grades. I expect you to have knowledge about programming and be highly motivated.
